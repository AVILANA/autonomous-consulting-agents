### Structural Technology Constraints of the Off-Price Model
The following are structural factors limiting technology adoption at TJX versus regular-price peers [INFERENCE]:
1. **No stable SKU catalog:** ~21,000 vendors with constantly changing, non-repeating inventory makes ML-based demand forecasting structurally harder
2. **No replenishment cycle:** TJX does not reorder the same item; every buy is opportunistic. Standard replenishment optimization tools don't apply
3. **Price asymmetry advantage:** The business model advantage is buying knowledge and relationships, not data infrastructure
4. **No loyalty program:** TJX does not operate a customer loyalty program — limiting personalization data and CRM capability vs. Nordstrom Rack or regular-price peers
5. **Treasure hunt is the product:** The lack of online inventory visibility is intentional — showing what's in store would undermine the discovery experience
