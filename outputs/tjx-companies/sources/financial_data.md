## FINANCIAL SNAPSHOT SUMMARY

**TJX is one of the world's most financially consistent large-cap retailers:**
- Revenue CAGR FY2022-FY2026 (post-COVID normalization): ~+6% per year [FACT/INFERENCE]
- Gross margin expansion: from 27.6% (FY2023 trough) to 31.0% (FY2026) [FACT]
- Operating leverage improving: operating margin 9.1% (FY2022) → 11.9% (FY2026) [FACT]
- No meaningful debt concern: $2.9B debt vs. $6.2B cash [FACT]
- Free cash flow: $4.9B in FY2026 on $60.4B revenue [FACT]
- Store count growth intact: +129 stores in FY2026, long-term target 7,000 stores [FACT]
