### body_brain_diagnosis.md
DEFINITIONS:
- Body = PHYSICAL network: warehouses, suppliers, factories, routes, DCs. Changing = opening/closing/moving. Expensive, slow.
- Brain = how you OPERATE within the network: processes, planning, systems, data, automation. Changing = improving/deploying/connecting. Faster, cheaper.
- KEY RULE: If you can change it without physically moving or closing something, it is BRAIN.

For each top stream: body evidence, brain evidence, primary bottleneck, recommended approach.
Also assess: velocity per supply chain area — can body keep up with brain speed?
